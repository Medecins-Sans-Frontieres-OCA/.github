## How to Use This Organization

*   Host your OCA-related repositories here instead of in personal or external accounts.
*   Create clear READMEs and documentation for your projects.
*   Use issues and pull requests to coordinate changes with colleagues.
*   Make repositories private, internal or public depending on sensitivity and purpose.
*   Follow security best practices and avoid storing confidential data.

***

## Collaboration Guidelines

To keep this space tidy and useful:

*   Document projects clearly (setup, purpose, architecture, dependencies).
*   Keep repos scoped to OCA needs and use cases.
*   Use consistent naming, branching, and review workflows.
*   Avoid committing secrets or sensitive operational data.
*   Engage constructively in reviews and discussions.
